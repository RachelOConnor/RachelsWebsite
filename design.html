Design

Peer Mentors Banner

This banner was made by myself and two others for the Peer Mentor Programme in SETU.
I'm currently part of this programme for the last 3 years, volunteering my time into university events and helping new students adjust.

Mary (the head of us Peer Mentors!) asked us to give her banner a refresh; something colourful and eyecatching!

The poster was created using Figma.

Computer Science Ball

I made these posters to promote my university's computer science ball, which turned out to be a great success!
I made these posters to print and stick around the university, and made separate designs for the social media posts, also used to promote the ball.

I created these posters in Canva.

Graphic Design Poster

I made this poster in second year for graphic design. I love cars so I wanted to make this poster about the history of a car, which I've chosen to be a Toyota GT86.

I made the font myself in Fontstruct, and created the visual assets in Aseprite.

Bronze Age Brawl

I made this poster to promote our game Bronze Age Brawl at the 2024 Games Fleadh.
This poster was also transferred to a zipup hoodie, which we wore to the event to promote the game even further!

I created this poster using Aseprite and Inkscape.

President's Volunteer Award Ceremony

This poster was made by myself and two others for our Volunteer Awards Ceremony.
This ceremony involves awarding those who volunteered their time into helping with events, where I won a gold award!
The poster on the left was my initial design, and the poster on the right is the final collective design.

Reconnect Fest

This poster was made for the Reconnect Fest held in both SETU Waterford and SETU Carlow.
The idea of the Reconnect Fest was to allow students to take a break from screens and connect with others through playing games and competitions on campus. It was a great success!

The posters on the left and in the middle were my initial designs, and the poster on the right is the final collective design.
